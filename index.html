Our Apps

Handwriting Generator

Our Intelligent Handwriting Generator converts your text into realistic animated video on a chalkboard, whiteboard or paper surface backgrounds. Customize your fonts, choose your backgrounds, integrate with your offsite content and even host your completed work - all for fraction of money and time charged by other platforms.

Typing Video Creator

Create photorealistic videos of typing in action with ability to bake in human like behaviors such as error correction, page changes or overwriting. Build narration ready content for educational tutorials, business presentations, social media or to improve collaboration. Choose to add or remove sound layers built intelligently matching the activity on the screen.

Text Animations

Always free to use, we provide easy to build animations that can be used to build engaging content and beautiful designs for any of your needs. Our animation generator app allows you to build and download animations like fade-in, pan, zoom, expand, scale text and images for daily design needs. Choose from our royalty free media or upload your own media.

Features

Code and Web Ready

All our applications run on modern browsers (eliminating the need to rely on any installed software or plugins) and are developer friendly from the ground up. With an easy to use markup language for predictable replication and allowing creators to save their animated videos as code.

Fully Customizable

Our software is designed to allow creators customize the outcome to suit their needs. Be it fonts, background imagery, language, video speed, resolution, colors and photo realistic effects.

Support and Consulting

Our team of experts and creators will guide you through your needs in getting your media or content completed. Premium customers can expect reliable service level agreement based turnaround times for their questions and queries. Community support is always free to use.
